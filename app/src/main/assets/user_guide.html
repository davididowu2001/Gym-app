2. Add exercises to your routine by browsing the available exercises.

Finding Nearby Gyms

1. To find nearby gyms, click on the 'Find Gym' button on in the settings menu.

2. The app will display a map with nearby gym locations, along with contact information and hours of operation.

3. Tap on a gym location to get directions or additional information.

Posting Your Workouts

1. To post your completed workouts, navigate to the 'Routines' tab on the navigation bar.

2. Select a workout routine from the list of routines you have created.

3. Click on the 'Start' button to begin your workout.

3. Click on the 'finish' button to end your workout.

4. After completing your workout, click on the 'Post Workout' button.

5. Your workout will be posted, and you can view it on the 'Posted Workouts' tab on the main screen.
